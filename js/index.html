Causes of Global Warming

Fossil Fuels

Burning coal, oil, and gas for energy releases massive CO₂ into the atmosphere.

Deforestation

Cutting down forests removes carbon sinks, accelerating greenhouse gas buildup.

Agriculture

Livestock farming produces methane, while fertilizers release nitrous oxide.

Industrialization

Manufacturing and construction processes emit significant greenhouse gases.

How You Can Help

Save Energy

Switch to LED lighting, unplug devices, and choose energy-efficient appliances.

Green Transport

Walk, bike, carpool, or use public transit to reduce your carbon footprint.

Reduce & Recycle

Minimize waste, reuse products, and recycle materials to lower emissions.

Plant Trees

Trees absorb CO₂ naturally. Support reforestation and plant in your community.
